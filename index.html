MeetScribe

Speech recognition has by far been one of the most powerful products of technological advancement. The purpose of speech recognition is to understand the voice of the speaker and the meaning of the spoken words. Speech recognition has the potential to replace the keyboard and make it unnecessary to type on the computer. MeetScribe is a powerful tool that helps students to convert meeting into text,so that if a student misses out on some important part they will have notes to go through again.

Technology Used

Html,CSS and Javascript has been used for frontend. And Azure services has been used for Qna service,speech to text translation and for Chat bot.
